Adeel Ahsan

Aerospace and Electrical Engineer

Hi, I'm Adeel, an aerospace and electrical engineer currently pursuing my Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering at the Intelligent Systems Lab, Memorial University of Newfoundland. My research focuses on optimal nonlinear, model-based, disturbance-aware predictive control, and trajectory generation for aerospace systems operating in complex, cluttered environments. When I'm not in the lab, you’ll likely find me exploring St. John’s by foot, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, hitting the gym, playing soccer, or enjoying a scenic hike.
